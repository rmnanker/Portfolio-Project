Professional Experience

In the summer of 2016 I interned at Boston Scientific as a Regulatory Affairs, Clinical Trials, and Medical Affairs Intern. This rotation allowed me to participate in a variety of projects and gain experience in multiple areas.

As a Regulatory Affairs intern, I authored a 30 Day Notice for manufacturing changes. This project included speaking with the engineer in order to understand the changes being made, writing the document, meeting with the Regulatory Specialist to ensure that everything would fulfill the FDA's requirments, submitting the notice to the FDA, and answering any questions the FDA had about the submission.

As a Clinical Trials intern I tailored the Informed Consent Forms for both the US and EU clinical trials of a combination product. My project as a Medical Affairs intern was to conduct a financial expentiture analysis of Investigator Sponsored Research. I also completed bonus projects during this internship which include updating a slidedeck including trial results for Medical Affairs and enhancing a Clinical Experience Summary by organizing product families per indicated clinical use to support global regulatory filings.
